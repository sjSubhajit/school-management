Privacy Policy for Onest Schooled Management System

Privacy Policy
Last updated: 22 November, 2025

Introduction
Onest Schooled Management System values your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

  • User Data: Names, email addresses, contact details, roles (student, teacher, parent, or admin).

  • Operational Data: Attendance, grades, homework, library usage, and fee transactions.

  • Device Data: Device type, operating system, and logs for app functionality.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To facilitate administrative operations, such as admission, fee collection, attendance, and academic management.

  • To provide personalized dashboards for students, teachers, and parents.

  • To enhance user experience and app security.

3. Sharing Your Information
We only share data with:

  • School administrators for operational purposes.

  • Authorities when required by law.

4. Data Security
We implement industry-standard security measures, including encryption and regular audits.

5. Your Rights

  • Access or modify personal data through the user portal.

  • Request deletion of your data by contacting support (subject to operational constraints).

6. Policy Updates
We may update this policy. Changes will be notified via email or app alerts.

Contact Us
For questions or concerns, reach us at sales.onesttech.com

School Management Software (SMS) is a digital solution designed to simplify and automate administrative, academic, and operational tasks in educational institutions. It serves as a centralized platform to manage activities such as student records, attendance, fee collection, staff management, academic scheduling, and communication with parents.
